Why I Built This

I've been doing nails professionally for years — including celebrity clients. I know what a real nail appointment should feel like. Not rushed. Not overbooked. Not a gamble on who shows up.

Porterville deserved a studio that actually operates at the level of what I was doing in bigger cities. ZLUX is that studio.

I built the membership model because I wanted my best clients to always have a guaranteed spot — and because I believe nail health is a long-term relationship, not a one-time transaction.

ZLUX runs on precision, privacy, and priority. I'm training my artists in the same methods I use. Every client who walks through our door gets the same standard — no exceptions.
