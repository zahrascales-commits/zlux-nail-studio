Our Story

About ZLUX

A studio built on the belief that beauty is a form of self-expression — and deserves to be treated as such.

The Beginning

Built for People Who Care About the Details

ZLUX was founded with a simple conviction: that a nail appointment could be as considered and personal as any other luxury experience. Too often, nail studios rush — a quick dry, a hurried polish. We decided to do the opposite.

We opened as an appointment-only studio to give each client undivided attention. No walk-in chaos, no waiting. Just space, skill, and a genuine conversation about what you want.

Today we serve a growing community of clients who value artistry, honesty, and the quiet luxury of being truly looked after.
