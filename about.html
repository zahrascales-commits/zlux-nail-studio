The Founder

My name is Zahra. I built this studio from scratch — not because it was easy, but because I kept walking into places that were supposed to be for nails and leaving with damaged skin, infections, and work I was embarrassed by.

I did celebrity nails. I did wedding nails. I did nails backstage at events where the room cost more per hour than most people make in a week. And through all of it, I realized one thing: the problem was never about access. It was about standard. Most nail artists were not trained to care. They were trained to be fast. And speed kills nail health.

I have wrist pain now. Chronic. The kind that doesn't go away. And instead of leaving the industry I love, I made a decision: I am going to build the studio I always deserved to work in. Where the standard is the same every single time. Where clients don't leave wondering if they got lucky — they leave knowing exactly what they came for.

Zola is not a salon. It's a nail membership studio. Meaning you don't just come in and leave. You belong here. You have a profile. You have a record. You have an artist who knows your nails better than you do. You have priority on every open slot — before the public ever sees the calendar.

I trained my workers myself. Everything they do in this studio — every technique, every product choice, every interaction — was taught to them directly by me. They're not here to cut corners. Neither am I.

You don't know Zola yet. But your nails are about to.

The Zola Method

What Makes Us Different

01
Organic Products Only

Every product used at Zola is personally vetted by Zahra. If there is a healthier option, we use it — regardless of cost. Most studios cannot say that.

02
Nail Health Over Speed

We do not rush. We do not overbook. There is no version of a Zola appointment where your nail health comes second to a schedule. That is not a standard most salons maintain.

03
Russian Technique

The Russian manicure technique is applied with expertise at every service. The precision it requires is not offered as an afterthought here — it is built into how we work.

04
Personal Client Tracking

Every member's history is tracked — services, sensitivities, shape preferences, allergies, nail growth progress. No other studio in this area operates with this level of continuity.

05
Quarterly Nail Assessments

Black Card members receive a quarterly nail assessment where Zahra personally reviews growth, health, and progress and builds a custom plan. This does not exist anywhere else.

06
Privacy as Standard

No walk-ins. No crowds. No waiting room. Privacy and precision are not features here — they are the baseline. Every client leaves feeling like the only person in the room.
