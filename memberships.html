Common Questions

Before You Join

How does the Member ID checkout work?

Enter your Member ID on the booking page to unlock your tier benefits. Whatever's included in your membership shows as $0.00. Anything outside your inclusions is charged at the regular price. Black Card add-ons show the 75% discount automatically.

Can I cancel anytime?

All memberships have a 6-month minimum. After 6 months you may cancel with 30 days written notice. No refunds on months already billed.

What happens if I miss a month?

Signature and Luxe services do not roll over. Black Card members may carry over 1 service per month. Your membership still auto-renews regardless.

Do I still pay a deposit with a membership?

Yes. Every appointment requires a 50% non-refundable deposit at booking. This protects the artist's time. Deposits on $0.00 service totals are $0.00.

Can I upgrade my tier?

Yes, subject to spot availability in the higher tier. Downgrades are not allowed until after the 6-month minimum.

How do I claim my Birthday Month Upgrade?

Enter your birthday month when booking or in your Client Portal. During your birthday month, the upgrade unlocks automatically and you'll see it as a free option at checkout. Available to all tiers — choose a free scrub, massage, or removal.
