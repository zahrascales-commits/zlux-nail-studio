Questions

Common Questions

What's included in a membership?+

Each tier includes a set number of free services per month — Signature gets 1, Luxe gets 2, Black Card gets 3. Add-ons, priority booking, and other perks vary by tier. All services count against your monthly allotment.

How does priority booking work?+

Black Card members get first access to every calendar opening. Then Luxe, then Signature, then guests. Your tier determines exactly when your booking window opens — the higher the tier, the earlier you get in.

Is there a contract?+

All memberships require a 6-month minimum commitment. After that, you're month-to-month with 30 days notice to cancel. The founding Black Card rate locks in forever at $299/mo the moment you join.

Can I roll over unused services?+

Services do not roll over month-to-month. Your membership renews on the same date each billing cycle — use your services within your period or they reset. Plan accordingly.

What's the cancellation policy?+

Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance can be rescheduled at no cost. Late cancellations or no-shows forfeit the service for that billing period. The 50% deposit collected at booking is non-refundable.

Do you take walk-ins?+

ZOLA does not take walk-ins. All services are by appointment only. Members always book before guests — if you want guaranteed access every month, a membership is the move.
